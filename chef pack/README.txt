                --== Norin's Toys ==--
More items for Lilith's Throne

Installation: Copy the contents of the included "res" folder into the game's "res" folder.


              --== This pack contains ==--
Weapons
	Chef's Knife
		A comfortable chef's knife, with a blade made out of ethereal energy. It's enchanted to help enhance the user's precision.
		Melee weapon, Sold by Vicky

Clothes
	Chef's toque
		A chef's toque, meant to keep the wearer's hair from falling into their cooking.
		Head slot, Androgynous, Sold by Nyan
	
	Chef's apron
		A traditional chef's apron made of thick cotton, with three large pockets.
		Over torso, Androgynous, Sold by Nyan

	Chef's jacket
		A traditional chef's jacket made of thick cotton, helping protect the wearer from hot liquids.
		Torso, Androgynous, Sold by Nyan
	
	Chef's short sleaved jacket
		A short sleaved chef's jacket made of thick cotton, helping protect the wearer from hot liquids.
		Torso, Androgynous, Sold by Nyan
